No-code setup

Claude Desktop, claude.ai & Cowork

Follow every step in order and you can't get it wrong. Takes about 3 minutes. You'll need Claude (app at claude.ai/download, or just claude.ai in a browser) and a Search Atlas account (free at searchatlas.com — you can also make one during sign-in).

1
Open Claude's Connectors page

Connectors are how Claude reaches outside tools. The link below opens the exact page — it works for both the Claude Desktop app and claude.ai (connectors are tied to your account, so they sync to both).

https://claude.ai/customize/connectors

Sign in to Claude if it asks. In the app you can also reach it via Settings → Connectors.

2
Find "Custom connectors"

Scroll all the way to the bottom of that page. Below the pre-built connectors (Gmail, GitHub, etc.) there's a section called Custom connectors — it's easy to miss, keep scrolling.

Click the + next to it, then Add custom connector.

!
Don't see it? You're likely on the wrong page. The URL must be claude.ai/customize/connectors. (Full fix in Troubleshooting below.)
3
Fill in the two fields

A small box pops up. Enter exactly this:

NameSearch Atlas (or anything you like)
Remote MCP URLhttps://mcp.searchatlas.com/mcp
https://mcp.searchatlas.com/mcp

Leave Advanced settings alone — Search Atlas handles sign-in automatically. Then click Add.

4
Sign in to Search Atlas (one time, ever)

The moment you click Add, a browser tab opens. Sign in to your Search Atlas account (or create one free), then click the big Authorize button.

✓
Connection done. You only sign in once — Claude remembers it from now on.
5
Turn it on in your chat — every new chat

Read this one twice — it's the step everyone forgets. Adding the connector does NOT switch it on automatically. In each chat where you want Search Atlas:

  • Click the + button next to the message box.
  • Choose Connectors (it may say "Add tools").
  • Toggle Search Atlas on.
!
You do this in every new chat. If Claude ever says it can't find Search Atlas tools, this toggle is almost always why.
6
Test it

Start a fresh chat, turn on the connector (Step 5), and send this:

Paste into ClaudeList my Search Atlas projects.

Claude calls a Search Atlas tool and shows your projects. If it's your very first tool call and you skipped Step 4, a sign-in tab pops up — click Authorize and you're set.

If something's wrong

Troubleshooting

Claude Desktop: I don't see "Custom connectors" or "Add custom connector"

Make sure the page URL is exactly claude.ai/customize/connectors — not Settings → Profile or Account. Scroll all the way down; custom connectors sit below the pre-built ones.

Custom connectors require a paid Claude plan (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise). On the free plan the option won't appear.

Team / Enterprise: your workspace owner must add it at the org level first — they go to claude.ai/admin-settings/connectors → Add → Custom → Web → paste https://mcp.searchatlas.com/mcp. After that it shows on your personal Connectors page.

Claude says it can't find Search Atlas / "no tools available"

Claude Desktop: you forgot to turn the connector on for this chat — click the + next to the message box → Connectors → toggle Search Atlas on. You do this in every new chat.

Claude Code: restart Claude Code after installing — the MCP loads on the next session start. Then check with claude mcp list.

The sign-in tab never appeared, or I got "Unauthorized"

Sign in at searchatlas.com first, then ask Claude to run a Search Atlas tool again and click Authorize. On Claude Code, your OAuth token may have expired — re-run any tool and re-authorize, or restart.

Claude Code: the slash commands didn't show up after install

Restart Claude Code. A restart activates everything and shows the welcome banner. To update later: /plugin marketplace update searchatlas then /plugin update searchatlas (run the marketplace update first).

Founding Kickoff Call
The vision call that set the foundation

Manick opened the AMM with a vision call that set the foundation for everything that followed. The session covered launching websites for agencies and personal brands using the agentic workflow — and ended with Manick's direct challenge to the group:

"This foundation that we just started on today — this is the thing that builds everything else. You're gonna take what we did today, and in two months from now, what you're gonna be able to do with this is at a crazy level, probably far beyond even what you would have expected. But this is how we're running all of our campaigns now. All of our team are kind of engineers now. If you're doing it in the UI versus doing it agentically, you just won't be able to keep up."

Key themes: launching agentic websites for agencies and brands, running campaigns the way SA's own team does, understanding that skill compounds — what seems hard today becomes instinct.
